#CECEFF Design Challenge

CAD and Fusion

We learned how to use the website of Fusion and CAD and made 6 different objects. CAD is the use of computer systems to aid in the creation, modification, analysis, or optimization of a design. CAD stands for Computer Aided Desinger. The task was to pick 6 objects out of 50 and create them on Fusion. I created these 6 designs. CAD gives you the oppurtunity to make anything and everything you want. From there you can put your creation into many different programs depending on what your printing.
